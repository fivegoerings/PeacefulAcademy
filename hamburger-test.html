Hamburger Menu Test

Resize your browser window to less than 768px width to see the hamburger menu.

On mobile devices or narrow screens, you should see a hamburger icon (three horizontal lines) in the top right corner.

Click the hamburger icon to toggle the mobile menu.

Test Instructions:

  1. Resize your browser window to be narrower than 768px
  2. You should see a hamburger icon (☰) appear in the top right
  3. Click the hamburger icon to open the mobile menu
  4. The menu should slide down from the top
  5. Click a menu item or outside the menu to close it
